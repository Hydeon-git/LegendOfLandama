Intro

Welcome to Landama's website!

"Dig in to the story to know who you are, and what's your destiny in this world!"

The game is about a Heroine who has arrived at this town to save the people of Landama from the villains and monsters. She will be helped by a Mage who will follow her where she goes. In the village she will be able to talk with some people and will find enemies to beat fighting in a frenetic Turn-based combat system. Go inside Landama's town and fight, make friends, save them from fear and become the Legend of Landama!

Features

Big and extense fantasy world where you can discover, explore, fight enemies, complete quests and a shop where you can buy anything you need to complete your journey.

Team members: While discovering and exploring Landama, the heroine will find and meet many characters. Some of them will join the heroine on her adventure to help her solve the puzzle and fight the enemies to save Landama.

Quests: Three different types of quests to complete. In order to get them you must talk with the people around the village. This quests will help the heroine understand the game and follow throughout the storyline.

Puzzles: Ingenious and creative puzzles you must complete in order to solve the main conflict of the game.

Turn-based Combat: The heroine will face many enemy battles from the beginning of the journey to the very end. This battles are developed in turns, and the heroine can decide to attack or defend on each turn. It's up to you to choose wisely before making your next move!

Social Interaction: Having dialogs with all non-playable characters is very important to follow and understand the plot.
